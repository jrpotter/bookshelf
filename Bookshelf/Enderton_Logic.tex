\section{\unverified{Lemma 0A}}%
\label{sec:lemma-0a}

Assume that $\langle x_1, \ldots, x_m \rangle =
  \langle y_1, \ldots, y_m, \ldots, y_{m+k} \rangle$.
Then $x_1 = \langle y_1, \ldots, y_{k+1} \rangle$.
